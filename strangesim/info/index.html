How to Explore the Simulation

  • Spacebar: Pause/Resume the simulation.
  • Z: Show/hide details.
  • X: Toggle rotation speed.
  • S: Take a screenshot.
  • Enter: Change to a random attractor.
  • Backspace: Clear all particles.
  • Alt: Add more particles.

Use the above keys to interact with the simulation and explore different behaviors of the attractors.
