Reading 4: My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge

You walk away from the loud building. Too much is happening there. Can't focus on one thing at a time. You walk away wondering what to do, how to show people who you are, and what you can do in your own controlled environment. You slowly build your own little tent, then it grows into a small hut, then a cabin. You created this place to organize your things. People come by sometimes, enjoying how you've organized everything together. There are many different rooms to explore, all under your control. You can create another cabin if you want, you can put however much effort you want into it and maintain it as you wish, but the option will always be there. This cabin is your website and yours alone.
