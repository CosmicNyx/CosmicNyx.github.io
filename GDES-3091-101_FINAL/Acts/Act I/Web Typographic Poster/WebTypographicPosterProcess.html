Web Typographic Poster

Coming Up With An idea

For my poster, I wanted to do something simple and use a minimal colour pallete. I sketched some gridi ideas and settled with 3 rows and two columns. One column will have the font typography I made with the hover animation I made for it.

Coding The Poster

I first coded the grid onto the page . I tried to make it one grid, but in the end I had to make 3 grids, each havijng 1 row and two columns. In total the page has 5 grids, one the holds the title, the one that holds the link, and the three that have the text.

To add more interactivity, I made a spoiler text where when you hover over it the text is revealed. I first made it on CodePen.
